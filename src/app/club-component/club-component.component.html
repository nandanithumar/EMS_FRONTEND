Chess


Chess is a board game for two players.
It is played in a square board, made of 64 smaller squares, with eight squares on each side.

Carrom


Carrom (also spelled carom) is a cue sport-based tabletop game of South Asian origin.
Carrom is very commonly played by families, including children, and at social functions.

Table Tennis


Table tennis, also known as ping-pong, is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using small rackets.
The game takes place on a hard table divided by a net.
